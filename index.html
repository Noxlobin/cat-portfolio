Kitten VS Sheep

A kitten just entered the ANIMAL PUBLIC JUNIOR SCHOOL by her mom! But unlike cat schools, this was filled with all sorts of animals. Dogs, giraffes, even the blue whale! In an attempt to win, the little kitty decided to fight a sheep! Yes, she was fighting a strong, big sheep with tiny cat muscles! Will she overthrow the sheep in order to be the school champion? Or will she be uncool forever? Find out!

Kitten Documentary

Hungry Kitten
